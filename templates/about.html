Our History.

"HISTORY HAS BEEN KIND TO US, BECAUSE WE HAVE ALWAYS INTENDED TO CREATE IT."

From a renovated chai Tapri in Ujjain, to far-off places with names we sometimes mispronounce, the journey that began in 1998 with 1 guys and the ice cream business they built is as legendary as the ice cream is euphoric.

Take a Stand for Children

Jitendra Ice-Cream joins in a cooperative campaign with the national non profit, Children's Defense Fund; the campaign goal is to bring children's basic needs to the top of the national agenda. Over 70,000 postcards are sent to Congress concerning kids and other national issues.
